Molten Lava Cake for Kat

Inspired by Kat for Valentines Day 2020

The very first dessert my partner made for me was a microwaved chocolate mug cake. In the years prior to this one, I've yet to make something or even celebrate Valentines Day with my partner since I worked in the restaurant industry. This year, my restaurant closed down and I ended up having time. Although we don't typically celebrate this day, I still haven't had the chance to say thank you for one of the finest desserts I've ever had. She spent Christmas in the Phillipines and brought home Davao Fine Tablea, which was something nostalgic to them in form of a thick hot chocolate. Here's the first dessert I've ever made for them:

Ingredients

  • Davao Fine Tablea
  • Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Eggs + 2 Yolks
  • 1/4th Cup Sugar
  • Salt
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Ice Cream, I used tuxedo from Rocky Point

Steps:

  1. I actually did not write down the steps
  2. Made a frozen ganache with the chocolate
  3. Made it like in the movie Chef
  4. This was two years ago but I'll try again this year, 2022
  5. Will be able to update this soon :)
  6. She said it was the best dessert she's ever had, and that's all I need to know.
  7. And we lived happily ever after.
