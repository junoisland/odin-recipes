I'm so Hangry

Han·gry

/'haNGgrē/

bad-tempered or irritable as a result of hunger.
"You should really eat. You're gonna get hangry again."

Working from home has given me the time to get lost in thought. Being able to allocate that energy is a skill. I dedicated my working life in the restaurant industry for 8 years. About a year ago, I decided to change careers since life aligned with the world's circumstances and ambitions of my own. I now work from home and find myself in a prediciment. Not having enough time, and having too much time on my hands. Operating in a tech-company has motivated me to be a student again. Learn a new skill, incorporate old passions and tie it together with your own goals.

My dream is to be a stay at home dad. I want my kids to eat healthy and to live long. I want them to taste the cooking of their future grandparents one day and what made me feel at home. I know there's still a long way to go, but I might as well sharpen my skills now.

For my future family,

May we come home to a good life, good company and good food.
